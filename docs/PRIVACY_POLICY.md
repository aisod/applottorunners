Lotto Runners (“**we**”, “**us**”, “**our**”) operates the Lotto Runners mobile and web application (the “**App**”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you use the App.

By creating an account or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

## 2. Who this policy applies to

The App serves different roles:

- **Customers** — people who request errands, transport, bus bookings, and related services  
- **Runners** — people who accept and complete jobs  
- **Administrators** — authorised staff who manage users, jobs, payments, and compliance  

This policy applies to all users of the App.


## 3. Information we collect

### 3.1 Information you provide

- **Account details:** name, email address, phone number, password (stored securely by our authentication provider; we do not store plain-text passwords)  
- **Profile information:** photo, addresses, user type (customer, runner, admin), and other details you choose to add  
- **Job and service data:** errand descriptions, pickup and drop-off locations, scheduled times, notes, ratings, and feedback  
- **Runner verification:** identity or compliance documents you upload for verification (where applicable)  
- **Communications:** messages sent through in-app chat or support channels  
- **Payment-related information:** transaction references, payment status, and amounts linked to jobs (payment card details are handled by our payment provider, not stored by us on our servers)

### 3.2 Information collected automatically

- **Location data:** GPS and approximate location when you grant permission — for example to pick addresses on a map, match runners to jobs, provide turn-by-turn navigation, and show live tracking during active jobs while you use the App (on iOS, limited background location may apply during an active runner trip when navigation or tracking is enabled)  
- **Device and usage data:** device type, operating system, app version, and general technical logs needed to operate and secure the service  
- **Notifications:** device tokens or identifiers used to deliver status updates about jobs, payments, and messages  

### 3.3 Photos, camera, and files

With your permission, the App may access your **camera** and **photo library** so you can attach images or documents to errands (for example shopping lists, prescriptions, or verification documents). Files may be stored on our cloud storage for the purpose of fulfilling and administering your request.

**Prohibited content:** You must not upload nudity, pornography, sexually explicit material, or sexual content involving minors. The App does not support public sharing of photos or messages; uploads are limited to service-related use and private job chat. See our [Terms of Service](TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md) (section 5) for full rules.


## 4. How we use your information

We use personal information to:

- Create and manage your account and authenticate you  
- Connect customers with runners and administrators  
- Process, assign, track, and complete errands and transport requests  
- Display maps, routes, distances, and live location during active jobs  
- Process payments and payouts, maintain wallets, and generate receipts  
- Send notifications about job status, payments, and account activity  
- Verify runner identity and compliance where required  
- Provide customer support and respond to feedback or complaints  
- Operate internal dashboards and operational reporting (for example job volumes and revenue summaries for administrators)  
- Improve reliability, security, and performance of the App  
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms  

**We do not sell your personal information** to third parties for their marketing purposes.

The App does **not** display third-party advertising networks and does **not** use separate consumer ad-tracking analytics SDKs (such as Google Analytics for Firebase or AdMob). Operational statistics shown inside the App (for example runner earnings summaries) are generated from your use of our service and data stored in our systems.

## 9. Security

We use technical and organisational measures appropriate to the nature of the data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls, and role-based permissions in our database. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Payment card and sensitive payment credentials are handled by **PayToday** (or your payment provider) under their own security standards.
