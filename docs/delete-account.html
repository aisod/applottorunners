This page explains how users of the Lotto Runners app (Android, iOS, and web) can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you only want to stop using the App, you may sign out instead of deleting your account.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (recommended)

Use this method if you can still sign in to Lotto Runners:

  1. Open the Lotto Runners app and sign in with your account.
  2. Go to Profile (profile icon or profile tab in the main navigation).
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete account.
  4. Read the warning, then type your email address exactly as shown to confirm.
  5. Tap Delete permanently and wait until the process completes (you will be signed out).

Before you delete: Finish or cancel any active jobs where you are the customer or runner. Deletion may fail or leave incomplete bookings if a job is still in progress.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app (for example you lost your device or cannot sign in), email us:

Email: info@lottoerunners.com

Subject line: Lotto Runners account deletion request

Include in your message:

  • The email address registered to your Lotto Runners account
  • Your full name and phone number (if on your profile)
  • A clear statement that you want your account and personal data deleted

We may ask you to verify ownership of the account before processing the request. We aim to complete verified requests within 30 days.

Email deletion request

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted successfully, we remove or disable access to personal data tied to your account, including:

  • Your login credentials (Supabase authentication account)
  • Your profile (name, email, phone, avatar, addresses, and role settings)
  • Errands where you are listed as the customer or runner
  • Transportation bookings linked to your user ID
  • Runner verification applications linked to your user ID

What data may be kept

Some information may be retained after account deletion where required by law or for legitimate business purposes, for example:

Payment & transaction records Amounts, dates, payment status, and references (e.g. PayToday) for completed or disputed transactions — kept for accounting, tax, and fraud prevention.
Support & legal records Copies of complaints, safety reports, or correspondence if needed to resolve disputes or comply with law.
Backups Encrypted backups may retain deleted data for a limited period before automatic purge (typically up to 90 days).
Aggregated analytics Non-identifying statistics (e.g. total jobs per month) that cannot reasonably be used to identify you.

Retention period: Records we must keep are stored only as long as necessary for the purpose above, or as required under the laws of the Republic of Namibia (often up to 7 years for financial records unless a shorter period applies).

Other data (for example in-app chat messages, uploaded files in cloud storage, ratings, or bus bookings) may be deleted with your account or removed on request; contact info@lottoerunners.com if you need confirmation for your account.
