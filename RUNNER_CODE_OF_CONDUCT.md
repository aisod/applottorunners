## 1. Service Commitment

I, the undersigned runner, agree to provide reliable, professional, and courteous service to all customers using the Lotto Runners platform. I understand that my conduct reflects on the reputation of Lotto Runners and affects the experience of all users.

## 2. Professional Standards

### 2.1 Punctuality
- I will arrive at scheduled times or communicate delays promptly
- I will provide accurate time estimates for service completion
- I will respect customers' time and schedules

### 2.2 Communication
- I will maintain clear, professional communication with customers
- I will respond promptly to messages and calls
- I will provide regular updates on service progress
- I will use respectful language at all times

### 2.3 Appearance and Behavior
- I will present myself in a clean, professional manner
- I will maintain appropriate personal hygiene
- I will conduct myself respectfully in all customer interactions
- I will avoid inappropriate language or behavior

## 3. Service Quality Standards

### 3.1 Errand Services
- I will complete errands efficiently and accurately
- I will follow customer instructions precisely
- I will handle items with care and respect
- I will provide receipts and documentation when requested
- I will report any issues or complications immediately

### 3.2 Transportation Services
- I will operate vehicles safely and legally
- I will maintain valid licenses and insurance
- I will keep vehicles clean and well-maintained
- I will follow all traffic laws and regulations
- I will ensure passenger safety at all times

## 4. Safety and Security

### 4.1 Personal Safety
- I will prioritize my own safety and well-being
- I will avoid dangerous situations or locations
- I will report any safety concerns immediately
- I will follow all safety protocols and guidelines

### 4.2 Customer Safety
- I will ensure customer safety during all services
- I will handle customer property with care
- I will maintain confidentiality of customer information
- I will report any suspicious activities

## 5. Confidentiality and Privacy

- I will protect customer personal information
- I will not share customer details with unauthorized parties
- I will respect customer privacy and confidentiality
- I will use customer information only for service delivery

## 6. Compliance and Legal Requirements

### 6.1 Legal Compliance
- I will comply with all applicable laws and regulations
- I will maintain all required licenses and permits
- I will pay all applicable taxes and fees
- I will report income accurately

### 6.2 Platform Compliance
- I will follow all Lotto Runners terms of service
- I will comply with platform policies and guidelines
- I will report violations of platform rules
- I will participate in platform training and updates

### 6.3 Commission and Payment Terms
- I understand and agree that Lotto Runners retains a platform service fee of **33.3%** (one-third) of every booking amount
- I will receive **66.7%** (two-thirds) of each completed booking as my earnings
- The commission structure applies to all types of bookings including:
  * Errand services
  * Transportation bookings
  * Contract subscriptions
  * Bus service bookings
- Commission is automatically calculated and deducted from each transaction
- I acknowledge that this commission covers:
  * Platform maintenance and development
  * Payment processing fees
  * Customer support services
  * Insurance and liability coverage
  * Marketing and customer acquisition
- Payment of my earnings will be processed according to the platform's payment schedule
- I will not attempt to circumvent the platform payment system or accept payments outside the app

## 7. Prohibited Activities

I understand that the following activities are strictly prohibited:

- Discrimination based on race, gender, religion, age, or other protected characteristics
- Harassment, intimidation, or inappropriate behavior
- Use of drugs or alcohol while providing services
- Fraudulent activities or misrepresentation
- Sharing of inappropriate content or communications
- Violation of customer privacy or confidentiality
- Operating vehicles without proper licensing or insurance
- Accepting payments outside the platform (except approved tips)

## 8. Customer Service Excellence

### 8.1 Customer Satisfaction
- I will strive to exceed customer expectations
- I will address customer concerns promptly and professionally
- I will accept feedback constructively
- I will work to resolve any service issues

### 8.2 Continuous Improvement
- I will seek opportunities to improve my service quality
- I will participate in training and development programs
- I will stay updated on platform features and policies
- I will contribute to platform improvement suggestions

## 9. Reporting and Accountability

- I will report any violations of this code of conduct
- I will cooperate with platform investigations
- I will accept responsibility for my actions
- I will participate in dispute resolution processes

## 10. Consequences of Violations

I understand that violations of this code of conduct may result in:
- Warning notices
- Temporary suspension of platform access
- Permanent termination of runner status
- Legal action if applicable
- Reporting to relevant authorities

## 11. Agreement and Signature

By signing below, I acknowledge that I have read, understood, and agree to comply with this Code of Conduct Agreement. I understand that this agreement is binding and that violations may result in termination of my runner status.

I also confirm that:
- All information provided in my application is accurate
- I have the legal right to work in this capacity
- I will maintain all required licenses and insurance
- I will comply with all applicable laws and regulations
- **I understand and accept the 33.3% platform service fee on all bookings**
- **I acknowledge that my earnings will be 66.7% of each completed booking**
